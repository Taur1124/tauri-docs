Help the community by resolving issues they consider worthy of a cash price!

<Steps>
1. ##### Create an account at Algora.io
   Tauri has chosen to utilize Algora.io in order to provide this bounty feature. In order to claim your reward you must [create an account on their platform](https://console.algora.io/).

1. ##### Find an issue you want to work on
   You can either check the list below or [visit Algora.io directly](https://console.algora.io/org/tauri-apps/bounties).

   Remember to check both the official bounties as well as the community bounties [here](https://console.algora.io/org/tauri-apps/bounties/community).

1. ##### Write the code!
   Bounties are collected by opening pull requests and getting them merged.
</Steps>
